Supplier Onboarding Policy
Version 1.0 — Issued 2025-09-01
Owner: Procurement Operations
Audience: Vendor managers and supplier success leads

Suppliers must complete security screening before receiving any production data.
Finance approval is required before onboarding suppliers with annual spend above 50000 USD.
Every supplier record must include an accountable business owner and a renewal date.
Tier-1 suppliers complete an annual on-site audit; Tier-2 suppliers complete a documentation-only review every two years.
Onboarding exceptions must be reviewed by Legal and Security within five business days.
Supplier records are retained in the procurement system for seven years after contract termination.
Procurement tracks open onboarding tickets weekly until finance signs off on every screening evidence file.
