Supplier Onboarding Policy
Version 2.0 — Issued 2026-02-15
Owner: Procurement Operations
Audience: Vendor managers and supplier success leads

Suppliers must complete security screening AND a privacy impact assessment before receiving any production data.
Finance approval is required before onboarding suppliers with annual spend above 25000 USD; the threshold dropped from 50000 USD in v1 after the 2025 audit.
Every supplier record must include an accountable business owner, a renewal date, and a documented data classification.
Tier-1 suppliers complete an annual on-site audit; Tier-2 suppliers complete a documentation-only review every eighteen months (down from two years).
Onboarding exceptions must be reviewed by Legal, Security, AND Privacy within three business days.
Supplier records are retained in the procurement system for ten years after contract termination.
Procurement reviews the open onboarding queue twice weekly with the supplier success lead until every ticket clears.
