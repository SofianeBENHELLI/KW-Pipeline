Supplier Onboarding Policy
Version 3.0 — Issued 2026-04-30
Owner: Procurement Operations
Audience: Vendor managers, supplier success leads, and the privacy office

Suppliers must complete security screening, a privacy impact assessment, AND an AI-Act self-attestation when the engagement involves automated decision-making.
Finance approval is required before onboarding suppliers with annual spend above 25000 USD; spend categorisation now distinguishes capex from opex.
Every supplier record must include an accountable business owner, a renewal date, a documented data classification, and a designated contractual contact.
Tier-1 suppliers complete an annual on-site audit; Tier-2 suppliers complete a documentation-only review every eighteen months; Tier-3 (limited-risk) suppliers self-attest annually.
Onboarding exceptions must be reviewed by Legal, Security, Privacy, AND the AI risk officer within three business days when the engagement is automated-decision-making.
Supplier records are retained for ten years after contract termination, with a litigation-hold override that pauses any deletion request.
Procurement runs a daily standup with supplier success during a tier-1 onboarding wave; otherwise the queue is reviewed twice weekly.
