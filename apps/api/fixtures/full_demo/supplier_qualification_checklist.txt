Supplier Qualification Checklist
Owner: Procurement Operations
Audience: Procurement reviewers and supplier business owners

Every new supplier must clear a security screening review and submit evidence of an information security program before activation.
Procurement validates the data classification of every supplier engagement and records the result in the vendor management system.
Tier-1 suppliers must publish an annual SOC 2 Type II report; Tier-2 suppliers may substitute a documented self-assessment.
Renewal dates and contractual contacts are mandatory fields; any record missing either is flagged for closure within five business days.
Onboarding exceptions are escalated to Legal, Security, and Privacy with a five-day review SLA.
Procurement maintains an audit trail for every screening, classification, and renewal decision.
