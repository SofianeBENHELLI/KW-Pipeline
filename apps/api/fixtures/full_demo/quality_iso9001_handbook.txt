Quality Management System Handbook
Owner: Quality Director
Audience: Operations, engineering, and audit teams

Acme operates an ISO 9001 quality management system across every engineering site.
ISO 9001 conformance is verified through annual surveillance audits performed by an accredited registrar.
Quality manuals capture process diagrams and ISO 9001 conformance evidence revised every quarter.
Surveillance audits log nonconformances against published manuals with severity codes and remediation owners.
Major nonconformances trigger a containment plan within ten business days and a root-cause corrective action.
Internal audits cover the same scope as the registrar's plan and feed the management review meeting.
Document control assigns every controlled record a unique identifier and a retention period of seven years.
The quality director chairs the quarterly management review and signs every revision of this handbook.
