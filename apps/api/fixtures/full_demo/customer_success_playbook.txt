Customer Success Renewal Playbook
Owner: Customer Success Operations
Audience: Customer success managers and renewal owners

Renewal risk is scored quarterly using usage signals, executive sponsorship, and engagement health.
A renewal risk score below 60 escalates to the customer success leadership team within five business days.
Every renewal cycle produces a Markdown summary with source lineage so Legal and Finance can review the same artifact.
Customer Success leadership reviews red renewal risk scores during the weekly leadership standup until the renewal closes.
The playbook references the procurement onboarding policy for any supplier review that blocks a renewal.
Quarterly business reviews include the renewal risk trend, the active escalations, and the Net Revenue Retention roll-up.
