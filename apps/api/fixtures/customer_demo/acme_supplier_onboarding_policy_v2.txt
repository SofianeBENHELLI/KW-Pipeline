Acme Supplier Onboarding Policy
Owner: Procurement Operations
Audience: vendor managers, supplier success leads, and finance reviewers

Suppliers must complete security screening before receiving production data.
Finance approval is required before onboarding suppliers with annual spend above 50000 USD.
All supplier records must include an accountable business owner, renewal date, and data classification.
Exceptions must be reviewed by Legal, Security, and Finance within three business days.
Quarterly supplier attestations are mandatory for suppliers handling regulated customer data.
