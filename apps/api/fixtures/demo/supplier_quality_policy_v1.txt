Supplier Quality Policy
Version 1.0 — Issued 2025-09-01
Owner: Quality Director, Global Operations

1. Purpose
This policy defines the minimum supplier quality requirements for materials
and components delivered to the manufacturing line. Compliance is mandatory
for every active supplier on the approved vendor list. The supplier scorecard
program tracks adherence on a quarterly basis.

2. Scope
This policy applies to all direct-material suppliers across the approved
vendor list. Indirect-services vendors and logistics carriers are covered by
a separate procurement policy and remain out of scope for supplier quality.

3. Inbound Inspection
Every inbound supplier lot is sampled per AQL 2.5 acceptance criteria and the
result is recorded in the receiving inspection log. Lots that fail inbound
inspection are quarantined within twenty-four hours and the supplier is
notified the same business day. Sampling plans follow ANSI/ASQ Z1.4.

4. Non-Conformance
A non-conformance report is opened for every quarantined supplier lot.
Suppliers have ten business days to submit a containment plan and a
root-cause corrective action against the non-conformance report. Repeat
non-conformance from the same supplier triggers an escalation review.

5. Supplier Audits
Tier-1 suppliers are audited annually on site against this supplier quality
policy. Tier-2 suppliers receive a documentation-only audit every two years.
Audit findings rated Major or Critical block further purchase orders to the
supplier until the corrective action is closed and verified.

6. Records and Retention
All inbound inspection records, non-conformance reports, and supplier audit
findings are retained for seven years in the quality management system. The
quality management system is the system of record for supplier quality
evidence requested during external regulatory audits.

7. Revisions
This supplier quality policy is reviewed annually by the quality director and
the head of supplier management. Revisions are tracked in the quality
management system change log.
