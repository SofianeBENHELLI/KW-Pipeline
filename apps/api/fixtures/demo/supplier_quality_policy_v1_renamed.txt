Supplier Quality Policy
Version 1.0 — Issued 2025-09-01

1. Purpose
This policy defines the minimum quality requirements for materials and
components supplied to the manufacturing line. Compliance is mandatory
for every active supplier on the approved vendor list.

2. Scope
Applies to all direct-material suppliers. Indirect-services vendors are
covered by a separate procurement policy and are out of scope here.

3. Inspection
Every inbound lot is sampled per AQL 2.5 and recorded in the receiving
log. Lots that fail inspection are quarantined within 24 hours and the
supplier is notified the same day.

4. Non-conformance
A non-conformance report is opened for every quarantined lot. Suppliers
have ten business days to submit a containment plan and a root-cause
corrective action.

5. Audits
Tier-1 suppliers are audited annually on site. Tier-2 suppliers receive
a documentation-only audit every two years. Audit findings rated Major
or Critical block further purchase orders until closed.

6. Records
All inspection, NCR, and audit records are retained for seven years in
the quality management system.

7. Revisions
This policy is reviewed annually by the quality director.
