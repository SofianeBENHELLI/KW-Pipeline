Supplier Quality Policy
Version 2.0 — Issued 2026-03-15

1. Purpose
This policy defines the minimum quality requirements for materials and
components supplied to the manufacturing line. Compliance is mandatory
for every active supplier on the approved vendor list and is verified
on a continuous basis through the supplier scorecard program.

2. Scope
Applies to all direct-material suppliers. Indirect-services vendors are
covered by a separate procurement policy and are out of scope here.

3. Inspection
Every inbound lot is sampled per AQL 1.5 and recorded in the receiving
log. Lots that fail inspection are quarantined within 8 hours and the
supplier is notified within the same shift. Repeat failures within a
rolling 90-day window trigger an automatic 100% inspection regime.

4. Non-conformance
A non-conformance report is opened for every quarantined lot. Suppliers
have five business days to submit a containment plan and ten business
days to submit a root-cause corrective action with effectiveness
verification.

5. Audits
Tier-1 suppliers are audited twice a year, alternating between on-site
and remote. Tier-2 suppliers are audited annually. Audit findings rated
Major or Critical block further purchase orders until the corrective
action has passed an effectiveness check.

6. Records
All inspection, NCR, and audit records are retained for ten years in
the quality management system. Records are exported nightly to the
corporate archive for disaster recovery.

7. Revisions
This policy is reviewed every six months by the quality director and
the head of supply chain.
